Gaurav-art is a modern contemporary designer and art-director from New Delhi, India who designs amazing footwear products with the uniqueness in mind.

In his words

I'm designing to inspire those people who dare to be different, the ones who don't follow the crowd. I design footwears with the uniqueness in mind, that help people to stand out.

mission

Unleashing every possibility of creative solutions, innovations and reaching the highest potential to create the most unique footwear designs.

vision

Deliver uniqueness through designs- designs that can make people feel different and get them inspire to stand out from the crowd.

core valuesTake inspiration from everywhere and everyone, but will not copy anyone.

Pursuit for perfection. and not settle for anything less than excellence.

Focus on the consumer, welcome feedback and suggestion
