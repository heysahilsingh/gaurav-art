Excellent Hand sketching ability and organizational skills, Visualization skills to express ideas in both 2D and 3D.

Responsible for selecting materials and executing color strategy, and design process from conceptualization to finished product. including technical specing, development, and costing.

Ability to respond quickly to multiple projects with strong time management skills and able to Analyzes and evaluates other designer and critiques professionally.

05

Efficiency to establish and maintain effective relationships with other designers, development team and staff, employees and the general public.

Development of Tooling, Gauges and Dies, Lasts. Familiar with all footwear-tools construction and machineries such as Lasted, PU, injection moulding, Vulcanized, Strobel, Stuckon etc.

PRODUCT DEVELOPEMENT

Conceptualizing and developing a technically sound sample which should be production friendly. Keep tracking on whole sample developments.

CORDINATING

Coordinating between Marketing Team and Development, to get the Physical samples closest to the requirement and feedback.

EXACT INFORMATION

Passing the 'exact information' needed for the factory to start production. Visit the production line if any potential problem occurs.

solutions oriented

10

Anticipating the problems that may arise during production, and designing best possible solution within limited resources.

product testing

11

Manage entire (product testing’s) and respective standards. Quality checks time to time in production line. (Cutting, stitching, closing, bottom, finishing)

handling issues

Handling Issues Related to Cost, such as cost negotiation and reduction, Commercialization, QC/QE. Vendor development and Material Sourcing. Range Planning, Order Processing and Scheduling.
